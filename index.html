About Me

Student

Hello! I'm a recent high school graduate (12th pass) with a passion for building new things, especially when they involve a good challenge. Whether it's composing music, getting lost in a captivating book (Urdu literature holds a special place in my heart!), or diving headfirst into learning new skills, I'm always eager to explore and create.  My recent portfolio website, built entirely with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript (even though I had no prior experience with them!), is a testament to my determination and love for learning.  However, my technical skills extend beyond web development.  I'm also a certified technician with expertise in laptop chip-level technology, demonstrating my ability to tackle complex problems with a hands-on approach. While I don't have a specific degree yet, I believe in the power of hard work and perseverance. If I set my mind to something, I'll find a way to make it happen.
